Welcome to Simplosophies!

Our goal here is as simple as our name:
To create a website where people can come click on random things and get positivity shoved into their face. We believe that the biggest problem in the world is the internet but not because the internet is bad. It's just being used badly. We hope that by reaching into the minds of the greatest and kindest thinkers of all time, we can share with you the way they believe we can all live happier lives, more peacefully, together.
